My Journey

From passion to profession

5+ Years Experience

My journey as a video editor began in 2015 when I first discovered the power of visual storytelling. What started as a hobby editing videos for friends quickly evolved into a deep passion for the craft.

After completing my degree in Film and Media Studies from New York University, I began working with local content creators, helping them bring their visions to life. Each project taught me something new, and my skills grew alongside my client base.

Today, I've had the privilege of working with over 50 clients worldwide, from independent artists to established brands. My work has been featured on major platforms and has collectively garnered millions of views.
