My Workflow

How we'll work together to create your perfect video

1

Consultation

We'll discuss your project goals, target audience, and creative vision to ensure we're aligned from the start.

2

Material Submission

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about my services

I can work with most common video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, and professional formats like ProRes and DNxHD. For best results, please provide the highest quality files available. If you're unsure about your footage, just ask and I'll help you determine the best way to share your files.

You can share files via cloud storage services like Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer. For very large projects, I can provide access to my secure FTP server. I recommend organizing your files in clearly labeled folders (e.g., "Main Footage", "B-Roll", "Audio Files") to ensure efficient workflow.

A round of revisions includes all the changes you request after reviewing a draft. This might include trimming sections, adjusting pacing, modifying text/graphics, or color correction tweaks. Major changes that require restructuring the entire video may count as additional rounds. I'll always clarify before proceeding if your requests might exceed the included revisions.

I have access to premium royalty-free music and sound effects libraries that I can use for your project at no additional cost. However, if you have specific music you'd like to use (original compositions or licensed tracks), you're welcome to provide those. For commercial projects, please ensure you have proper licensing for any provided music.

Absolutely! If you have reference videos or a particular style in mind, please share them with me during our initial consultation. I can analyze the editing techniques, pacing, transitions, and effects used in your examples and incorporate similar approaches into your project while maintaining originality.

Have more questions? Contact me directly and I'll be happy to help!
